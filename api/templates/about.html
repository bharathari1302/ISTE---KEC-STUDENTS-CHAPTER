About Us

Pioneering technical excellence and leadership development for a better tomorrow.

The Mission

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country. We operate with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

Our Chapter

At ISTE-KEC, we are a dynamic community committed to fostering technical excellence, professional growth, and innovative thinking among students and faculty members alike. Our chapter serves as a beacon of knowledge and collaboration.

Network & Growth

Being a part of ISTE-KEC opens doors to a vast network of professionals and resources, both at the national and international levels. Members benefit from exclusive access to technical resources, certifications, and opportunities.

Vision

To be a catalyst for change in technical education, empowering students to become industry leaders and innovators who can solve complex global challenges.
